It Is The Best Time Ever To Build

It is the best time ever to build something, or to have something built.

Greenfield apps that three years ago would have cost tens of thousands of euros, and would never have produced an ROI, are now being built for a few thousand euros. This creates opportunities for niches and applications that three years ago were not economically viable.

Before, tech was a frustration, a bottleneck. Now it is an opportunity. For everyone.

Tech is becoming easier, and therefore also more present, more abundant.

The number of opportunities for engineers is going to increase exponentially.

Because everyone can build now. But not everyone has the time and desire to:

  • manage
  • do the last 20%
  • dot the i's
  • do the security
  • ...

Tech presence in our lives will explode, and someone has to build and maintain it.
