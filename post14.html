Writing Vertical


I think it's fascinating to see people read traditional Japanese vertically in these little pocket books.

I wondered if it would also look cool in English

So I wrote a little App Script to try

The short answer, it doesn't look cool because English words are not equal in length

But I had a lot of fun trying and playing with it. I converted a famous essay into a vertical format for you to have a look. You have to read from top right to bottom left. Have fun!
