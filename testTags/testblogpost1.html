Principles

How would my 70 year old self think about my decisions?

What would he like my principles to be, for the rest of my life?

My grandfather died saying he had a fantastic life. I want to look back when I’m about to die and say that I had a fantastic life.

So what is a fantastic life?

"A fantastic life is one that brings me closer to my goals, and helps me live according to my principles."

Below are the principles.

Think long term

My 70 year old self wants me to play long term games, with long term people.

President Zelensky asked not to have a picture of him in government buildings, classrooms, ...

But instead for everyone to have a picture of their children. And think about them when making decisions.

Time is my most important resource in life

I don't want to waste it.
