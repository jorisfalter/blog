Get out of the way of people delivering the work

The incentive for Product and Project Managers is to inject themselves as much as possible in the business:

  • Have lots of meetings with many people to discover synergies
  • Setup one way of working which everyone has to follow, to make progress tracking easy, and uphold an appearance of discipline
  • Do regular follow ups with the team, to “know everything” to answer questions from management
  • Try to make big changes because changes attract the attention from senior management

My approach has been quite opposite, and in my opinion it has resulted in faster delivery of projects and significantly more motivated team members.

In short, my philosophy has two pillars:

  • Get out of the way of the people delivering the work
  • Write excellent reporting
