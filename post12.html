Get out of the way of people delivering the work

The incentive for Project Managers is to inject themselves as much as possible in the business:

  • Have lots of meetings with many people to discover synergies
  • Setup one way of working which everyone has to follow to make progress tracking easy.
  • Do regular follow ups with the team, to be able to “know everything” and being able to answer questions from management
  • Try to make big changes because changes attract the attention from senior management

My approach up to now has been quite opposite, and in my opinion it has resulted in:

  • - faster delivery of core projects
  • - significantly more motivated team members

In short, my philosophy has two pillars:

  • - get out of the way of the people delivering the work
  • - write and do excellent reporting
